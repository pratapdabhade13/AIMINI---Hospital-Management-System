Our Hospital Facilities

Modern Infrastructure | Advanced Equipment | Patient-Centric Care

Explore Our Facilities

1. Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Our ICU is equipped with the latest monitoring systems and life-support equipment. It is managed by expert critical care specialists and nurses available round the clock to provide emergency support to patients with severe health conditions.

2. Advanced Operation Theatres

We have fully modular operation theatres designed for complex surgeries. They are equipped with modern surgical instruments, anesthesia machines, and sterile environments ensuring patient safety and successful operations.

3. Diagnostic & Laboratory Services

Our diagnostic center provides X-ray, CT scan, MRI, blood tests, and other advanced medical tests. Accurate and quick results help doctors make better treatment decisions for patients.

4. 24x7 Pharmacy

The in-house pharmacy is open 24 hours, providing all prescribed medicines and essential healthcare products at affordable rates to patients and their families.

5. Emergency Ambulance Service

We provide fast and reliable ambulance service with trained medical staff. Our ambulances are equipped with oxygen support, stretchers, and first-aid facilities for patient safety during transit.

6. Comfortable Patient Rooms

We offer clean, air-conditioned, and well-furnished patient rooms with modern amenities. Each room is designed to provide comfort and care for both patients and attendants.

7. Maternity & Child Care Unit

Our maternity ward ensures safe deliveries and complete postnatal care for mothers and newborns. Pediatric specialists are available to provide expert care for infants and children.

8. Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Center

We provide personalized physiotherapy sessions for patients recovering from surgeries, injuries, or chronic pain. Our trained therapists focus on physical recovery and wellness.

9. Blood Bank

Our hospital maintains a licensed and safe blood bank, ensuring availability of all major blood groups in emergencies, with proper testing and storage facilities.

10. Cafeteria & Waiting Lounge

A hygienic cafeteria serves healthy food and refreshments for visitors. Comfortable waiting areas are provided with proper ventilation and seating arrangements.
